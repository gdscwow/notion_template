What is GDSC WoW?
GDSC WoW is a collaborative effort by GDSCs all around India. You can bring leads from cities all over the world to a larger location where you can network, share, and contribute. WoW used to happen for the past 2 years online. This year we feel it should be offline. We all hear about DevFests by GDGs but we as GDSC want to have an established name and we are planning to organise a seminar sort of.
How is GDSC WoW different from DevFest?
DevFest focuses on technologies and what is cheaper and more efficient. That means they focus on what but not why. WoW focuses more on the why part for the students and encourages solution-building for the communities. Why and what should proceed on. There will be a specific structure for WoW in each city with a little bit of flexibility where the regional leads can decide what more they want for their region's community.
HotSpots
How do I add my city to the list of HotSpots? For adding a new city there are some rules like checking the feasibility, for example, the number of attendees, the strength of the community and their proximity to your location, and the speculated number of attendees on the day of the event. After analyzing all these parameters, we encourage you to fill out the Phase 2 Form, whose link is given below: https://s.gdscwow.team/P2F
Phase 2 Form
Do I need to fill Phase 2 Form If I have already filled Phase 1 (Interest) Form? Yes, you need to fill out the Phase 2 Form even if you have already filled out the Phase 1 (Interest) Form. The reason for this being we have started sorting the leads based on their region as well to begin the region-wise planning and execution.
What are the steps after filling Phase 2 Form?
Based on the cities that you opt for, it will be easier for the other leads to recognize and collaborate with you and make your local event a greater success. After you have filled out the Phase 2 Form, we will review the details and will get back to you soon!
