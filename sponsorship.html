Sponsorship
GDSC WOW (Wonder of Wonders) is an offline tech event consisting of speaker session, hands- on session , job fairs and a virtual hackathon covering various topics of the latest technologies.
A collaborative event of 200+ GDSCs (Google Developer Student Clubs) from India, who have joined hands to bring together students, developers, and communities under the same roof.
